About this Activity

Experience the magic of Luxor from the sky. Drift peacefully over the Valley of the Kings, the Temple of Hatshepsut, and the Colossi of Memnon as the sun rises over the Nile River. This once-in-a-lifetime adventure offers unparalleled panoramic views of ancient Thebes.

Your journey begins with a pre-dawn pickup from your hotel or cruise ship. You’ll cross the Nile to the West Bank where coffee, tea, and snacks await as you watch the balloon inflation process. Then ascend gently into the morning sky for a 45-minute flight guided by expert pilots.
